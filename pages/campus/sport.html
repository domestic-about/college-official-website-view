The 61st Sports Meeting of Huazhong Agricultural University: School of Animal Science and Medicine won the first gold medal

On the morning of October 21, the 61st Huazhong Agricultural University Games kicked off at the East Stadium. School leaders Gao Chi and Li Zhaohu, as well as members of the school sports committee, staff representatives, and student representatives attended the opening ceremony. The women's football team of the School of Animal Science and Technology won the 2022 "Lion Rock Cup"

On the evening of May 27, the women's final of the 2022 "Lion Rock Cup" football match kicked off at the football field of the South Stadium. The women's football team of the School of Animal Medicine and the School of Life Science and Technology, who entered the final together, met on a narrow road. In the end, the women's football team of the School of Animal Science and Technology defeated the women's football team of the School of Life Science and Technology 2:0 in the rain and won the championship, and the women's football team of the School of Life Science and Technology won the runner-up.

The women's basketball team of the School of Animal Medicine defeated the women's basketball team of the School of Plant Sciences 38:20 and won the third place

On the morning of May 21st, the "Lion Rock Cup" women's basketball third place competition started in the West Gymnasium. Dai Jingui, deputy secretary of the Party Committee of the School of Animal Sciences, undergraduate counselors Wang Yuntao, Tan Liming, Cheng Tangyu, Gao Hongming, and fans from both schools watched the game. After the whole exciting contest, the women's basketball team of the School of Animal Medicine defeated the women's basketball team of the School of Plant Sciences with a big score of 38:20, and won the third place of the "Lion Rock Cup" women's basketball team

The men's basketball team of our school lost the runner-up

On the afternoon of May 22, the 2022 "Lion Rock Cup" basketball competition for postgraduate men's finals was held in the West Gymnasium. The School of Economics and Management and the School of Animal Medicine met on a narrow road. In the end, the School of Economics and Management defeated the School of Animal Science and Technology 58:54 to win the championship, and the School of Animal Science and Medicine won the runner-up.
