Student Activities

The Chorus of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine won the first prize in the "May Flower Sea" College Student Choral Competition

Learn from Lei Feng, Foster a New Spirit": The Sunshine Volunteer Service Team of the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine conducted the Lei Feng Month Volunteer Activities at Mingxing Kindergarten.

The opening match of the "Lion Mountain Cup" basketball tournament took place at the basketball court of the West Sports Center, with the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine competing against the College of Fisheries.

The Sunshine Volunteer Service Team of the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine carried out the "Lei Feng Month" volunteer activities at Star Kindergarten.

The College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine held the 8th "Unleash Your Creativity - Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine Mascot" Design Competition.

Spreading Cuteness with Love, Handmade Crafts for Animal Care: The Sunshine Volunteer Service Team of the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine organized a charity sale to support and care for animals.

The College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine achieved excellent results in the 2022 "Lion Mountain Cup" Freshman Table Tennis Tournament
On October 8th, the 2022 "Lion Mountain Cup" Freshman Table Tennis Tournament of Huazhong Agricultural University was successfully held at the East Sports Hall. The tournament was jointly organized by the Communist Youth League Committee of Huazhong Agricultural University, the Publicity Department of the University Party Committee, the Undergraduate School, and the Sports Department. It was open to all first-year students of the university.
Class 2102 of the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine organized a themed public welfare activity called "Handmade Transformation: Turning Waste into Treasure."
To enhance the environmental awareness among students and promote the virtue of frugality in the Chinese culture, on the afternoon of September 19th, Class 2102 of the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine organized a voluntary activity with the theme "Handmade Transformation: Turning Waste into Treasure" along Huwu Main Avenue. The main focus of this activity was to create flower vases through handcrafting, guiding students and teachers to utilize everyday items to create useful objects and turn waste into treasure.
Hold high the banner to honor the great, and sing the red songs to celebrate a prosperous era.
On the evening of September 19th, Huazhong Agricultural University held the New Students' Song Festival titled "Youth Dedication to the Top 20 Strong Nations, Our New Journey" at the East Sports Field. The 12th Group of the College of Animal Science and Technology and the College of Veterinary Medicine showcased their patriotic spirit by singing the song "Defend the Yellow River," while another group chose the song "When That Day Comes" to express their bright vision for the Top 20 Strong Nations.
