    Research Projects

    In the past five years (2018-2022), the college has undertaken 711 research projects, including 168 funded by National Natural Science Foundation of China, with newly approved funds of 543 million RMB for science and technology. 1 project has won National Science and Technology Invention Award, 17 have won provincial and ministerial prizes (including cooperation projects), 399 have been granted patents, and 9 new veterinary drug certificates have been approved.
