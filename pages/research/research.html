Research Introduction

The college currently has 6 national-level research institutions, 1 collaborative innovation center, 3 provincial-level international science and technology cooperation bases, and 18 provincial and ministerial-level research institutions. It has been approved to establish the National Experimental Teaching Demonstration Center for Veterinary Medicine, the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Agriculture's Wuhan Swine Agricultural Science and Education Cooperation Talent Training Base, and the Animal Science and Technology University Student Innovation Activity Base of Hubei Province. From 2011 to 2017, a total of more than 1600 papers of various types have been published, including nearly 1200 SCI-indexed papers. The average annual number of SCI papers is 169, and the highest impact factor of a single paper is 32.438. Among them, there are more than 10 SCI papers with an impact factor above 10. A total of 13 achievements have been identified, and 55 various types of science and technology awards have been received at all levels. Among them, the college has won 1 first prize and 1 second prize of the National Science and Technology Progress Award as the first completion unit, 1 second prize of the National Science and Technology Invention Award, 1 Outstanding Contribution to Science and Technology Award in Hubei Province, 7 first prizes and 2 second prizes of Hubei Province Science and Technology Progress Award, 2 second prizes and 1 second prize of Hubei Province Technical Invention Award. It has been granted 218 authorized patents, obtained 11 new veterinary drug registration certificates, formulated 12 national standards, and 5 local standards.

The School of has a total of 38 research platform bases at the provincial, ministerial, and national levels. Among them, there are 10 national-level platform bases, including the National Key Laboratory of Agricultural Microbial Resources Exploitation and Utilization (joint), the National Livestock Engineering Technology Research Center, the National Innovation Platform for Biological Breeding Integration (Swine), the Frontier Science Center for Animal Breeding and Healthy Farming, the National Innovation Sub-center for Digital Livestock Farming (Swine), the National Laboratory for Veterinary Drug Residue Standards, the National Laboratory for Veterinary Drug Safety Evaluation, the Experimental Animal Center of the National Bio-industry Base at Huazhong Agricultural University, the National Engineering Laboratory for Animal Disease Prevention and Control, and the National Special Laboratory for Animal Tuberculosis (Wuhan), among others.

There are also 5 international scientific and technological cooperation platform bases, including the International Joint Research Center for Animal Disease Prevention and Control, the International Joint Research Center for Animal Genetics, Breeding and Reproduction, the International Science and Technology Cooperation Base for Animal Breeding and Disease Control Technology, the International Joint Research and Training Center for Veterinary Epidemiology, and the "Science and Technology Innovation in China" and "Belt and Road Initiative" Innovation Institutes for Livestock Farming and Disease Control.
