    CHEN HUANCUN

    Preventive Veterinary Medicine

    Position Title: Professor, Academician

    Office Address:The Cooperative Innovation Center for Sustainable Pig Production Room 401

    Email :chenhch@mail.hzau.edu.cn

    Course Teaching: 《Veterinary Infectious Disease》

    Personal Profile

    Chen Huanchun, born in Enshi, Hubei province, is the professor of Huazhong agricultural university, supervisor of Ph.D students, and now the director of the Cooperative Innovation Center for Sustainable Pig Production, Hubei. Prof. Chen has been honored with many awards, including the State Council Special Allowance, Heliang & Heli Foundation for Scientific and Technological Progress Award, China Agricultural Talent Award, Award for the Outstanding Contribution to the National Science and Technology Project during the Eleventh Five-Year Plan Period, and the Award for the outstanding contribution to Science and Technology in Hubei Province, etc.

    Prof. Chen received his Doctor’s degree in Veterinary Medicine from University of Munich, Germany. Currently, he is the President of the >Chinese Veterinary Association, while also holds the positions in the Director of Academic Committee of the State Key Laboratory of Agricultural Microbiology; the Director of Academic Committee of the State Key Laboratory of Veterinary Biotechnology of Harbin Veterinary Research Institute, >Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences; the Chairman of Higher Learning Teaching Guidance Committee in the Majors of Animal Production, Ministry of Education; the expert committee member in State Council Emergency Handling Department; the member of Expert Committee of Ministry of Agriculture Science and Technology Committee; the member of Expert Committee of Science and Technology Commission of Ministry of Education; the member of the New Veterinary Drug Evaluation Committee of Ministry of Agriculture; Vice president of Genetical Modification and Technology of Ministry of Agriculture.

    By 2022, Prof. Chen has held more than 50 important scientific research projects sponsored by National Natural Science Foundation, “973” project, “863” project and national support programs. He has developed more than 30 kinds of genetically engineering vaccines and molecular diagnostic kits, and obtained 20 genetically modified certificates, 30 certificates of new veterinary drug registration, 53 invention patents, and 5 national certificates for the new and key products. Prof. Chen has formulated three national standards and the Ministry of Agriculture industry standards. Prof. Chen also won the 2nd prize of National Science and Technology Progress for four times, the 1st prize of Hubei Provincial Science and Technology Progress for six times, and the 2nd prize of national teaching achievement for two times. More than six-hundred research papers have been published by Prof. Chen’s team, within which over two-hundred ones were indexed by SCI. Besides, Prof. Chen has published three monographs as chief editor, and another three ones as the joint editor. Till 2022, there are 102 doctors’ and 120 masters’ students graduated under his education. The research group led by Prof. Chen has been selected into the Innovation Groups of the National Natural Science Foundation of China.

    Education Experience

    1984.10 – 1989.01, Ph.D. Veterinary Medicine, University of Munich, Germany

    Working Experience

    1989.02—1993.09, Lecturer, Associate Professor, Huazhong Agricultural University

    1993.10—1994.10, Visiting Research, University of Munich, Germany

    1994.11—2002.12, Professor, Supervisor of PhD students, Dean of College of Animal Science and Technology, and College of Veterinary Medicine, Huazhong Agricultural University

    2003.01—2007.12, Professor, Vice President of Huazhong Agricultural University, Academician of >Chinese Academy of Engineering, China

    2008.01—Till now, Professor, Academician of >Chinese Academy of Engineering, Huazhong Agricultural University1

    Research Area

    Veterinary infectious disease

    Development of novel veterinary vaccines and molecular diagnostic products
