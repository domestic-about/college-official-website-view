    Animal science

    The first batch of national first-class undergraduate professional construction sites. National Outstanding Agriculture and Forestry Talents Program

    This major is a national characteristic major and a brand major in Hubei Province. It cultivates students to master the basic theories and basic knowledge of animal genetics and breeding, animal reproduction, animal nutrition and feed processing, animal production and management, ecological intelligent breeding, etc., and receive basic training in investigation, analysis, evaluation, design and other aspects related to animal science. It has the basic ability to engage in animal genetic breeding, reproduction, nutrition, production and management, development and research, and can be competent in teaching, research, production, promotion, management and other work in the fields of animal genetics and breeding, animal nutrition and feed, special economic animal breeding, animal behavior and welfare, animal production and management, breeding environment control in the livestock industry.

    Veterinary medicine

    The first batch of national first-class undergraduate professional construction sites

    This major is a national characteristic major and a brand major of Hubei Province. It cultivates students to master the basic theories and basic knowledge of basic veterinary medicine, preventive veterinary medicine, clinical veterinary medicine, veterinary pharmacology and public health, and carry out basic training in animal body normal and abnormal structure and function experiments, inspections, disease prevention, diagnosis, treatment technology, animal product inspection and quarantine, etc. It has the basic ability to engage in animal health care, clinical diagnosis and treatment, prevention recommendations, animal product safety supervision and veterinary health management work. It can be competent in production, operation, management, technical research and development and promotion of veterinary clinical diagnosis and treatment, animal disease prevention and control, food safety and public health management in veterinary administrative management departments, veterinary clinical institutions, enterprises and institutions.
