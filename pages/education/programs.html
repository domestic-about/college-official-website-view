Introduction to Talent Development Projects

Focusing on the new positioning of talent cultivation in the new agricultural science, we will integrate all faculty members from schools such as chemistry, information technology, engineering, economics and management, and grammar, and collaborate with industry leading enterprises to establish talent cultivation projects for ecological and intelligent breeding, advanced clinical veterinarians, and advanced veterinary medicine creation. We will integrate general and specialized fields, cross disciplinary fields, and integrate the cultivation of urgently needed management talents for industry development.

1.Ecological and Smart Breeding Innovation Class

Cultivate and adapt to the fourth global new technological transformation in the new era (represented by big data, artificial intelligence, quantum information technology, virtual reality, and biotechnology), the transformation and upgrading of the agricultural and animal husbandry industry, and the construction of new agricultural sciences; Applied and compound high-level talents with a reasonable professional knowledge structure, solid foundation, high industry loyalty, and a sense of patriotism and significant integrated innovation awareness and ability.

2. Advanced Clinical Veterinary Innovation Class

Focusing on the new positioning of talent cultivation in the new agricultural science, we aim to cultivate outstanding veterinary talents that the industry needs for development. Facing veterinary clinical practice, we aim to cultivate professional, high-quality, innovative and internationally competitive senior clinical veterinary and industry management talents.Focusing on the new positioning of talent cultivation in the new agricultural science, we aim to cultivate outstanding veterinary talents that the industry needs for development. Facing veterinary clinical practice, we aim to cultivate professional, high-quality, innovative and internationally competitive senior clinical veterinary and industry management talents.

3.Advanced Veterinary Medicine Innovation Class

Focusing on the new positioning of talent cultivation in the new agricultural science, we aim to cultivate outstanding veterinary talents that the industry needs for development. Targeting veterinary biopharmaceuticals, we aim to cultivate professional, high-quality, innovative, and internationally competitive senior veterinary pharmaceutical engineering and industry management talents.
