Personal profile

Chen Huanchun, male, is from Enshi, Hubei. Professor and doctoral supervisor of Huazhong Agricultural University, director of Collaborative Innovation Center for Pig Healthy Breeding. Winner of Special Government Allowance of the State Council, Ho Leung Ho Lee Foundation Science and Technology Progress Award, China Agricultural Excellence Award, "Eleventh Five-Year" National Science and Technology Plan Implementation Outstanding Contribution Award, Hubei Provincial Science and Technology Outstanding Contribution Award. Doctor of Veterinary Medicine, University of Munich, Germany. He is currently the president of the Chinese Veterinary Medical Association, the director of the Academic Committee of the State Key Laboratory of Agricultural Microbiology of Huazhong Agricultural University, the director of the Academic Committee of the State Key Laboratory of Veterinary Biotechnology of the Harbin Veterinary Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, and the animal production major of the Teaching Steering Committee of the Ministry of Education. Chairman of the Teaching Steering Committee (2013), member of the State Council Emergency Response Expert Committee, deputy leader of the 973 Infectious Disease Expert Group of the Ministry of Science and Technology, deputy leader of the 863 Modern Agricultural Technology Expert Group (2017), member of the Expert Committee of the Science and Technology Committee of the Ministry of Agriculture , a member of the Expert Committee of the Science and Technology Committee of the Ministry of Education, a member of the New Veterinary Drug Review Committee of the Ministry of Agriculture, and a deputy chief engineer of the transgenic special technology of the Ministry of Agriculture. Presided over more than 50 major scientific research projects such as the National Natural Science Foundation of China, 973 (2006, 2010), 863, and national support programs, developed more than 30 genetically engineered vaccines and molecular diagnostic kits, and obtained 19 transgenic safety certificates, 24 New veterinary drug registration certificates, 49 invention patents, 5 national key new product certificates, 4 second prizes of the National Science and Technology Progress Award, 6 first prizes of the Hubei Provincial Science and Technology Progress Award, and 1 second prize of the National Teaching Achievement Award. Published more than 600 papers in domestic and foreign journals, more than 200 papers were included in SCI, published 3 monographs, participated in the editing of 3 books, and trained 95 graduates with doctorates and 112 masters. The research team led by him was selected into the innovation group of the National Natural Science Foundation of China.
