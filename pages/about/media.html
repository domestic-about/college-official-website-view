The College of Horticulture and Forestry Science is focused on high-quality research and top talent cultivation as the fundamental task of higher education. The college aims to develop cutting-edge research teams to address major issues in the horticulture industry and to meet the national needs for socio-economic and industrial development. We carry out basic to applied research based on modern science in key laboratories and engineering centers.
