The 6th "Huanchun Fund" Award Ceremony for Outstanding Teachers and Students and the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine Enterprise Scholarship Award Ceremony, along with the 21st Entrepreneur Forum, were held. .

On the afternoon of June 12th, the 6th "Huanchun Fund" Award Ceremony for Outstanding Teachers and Students, along with the 2023 College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine Enterprise Scholarship Award Ceremony, was held in the F101 Lecture Hall of the Fourth Comprehensive Building. Chen Huanchun, an academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, and Liao Jizhong, Deputy Secretary of the Party Committee and Secretary of the Discipline Inspection Commission, attended the award ceremony and delivered speeches. More than 400 representatives from supporting units of the "Huanchun Fund" Award, donating units of the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine Enterprise Scholarship, relevant university departments, and faculty and students of the College of Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine participated in the conference.
