The latest news from the College of Animal Science and Technology, Huazhong Agricultural University

The School of Animal Science and Technology launched a training session on knowledge graph and AI course construction

On the morning of July 19, the School of Animal Medicine held a training meeting on knowledge graph and AI course construction in the D202 conference room of the fourth complex building. Invited Li Siqi, a gold course consultant of Wisdom Tree Network Hubei Province, to give a report on "Construction of Knowledge Graphs, AI Empowering First-Class Disciplines and New Forms of Courses". The event was hosted by Ren Zhuqing. Li Siqi described the importance and necessity of knowledge map construction from four aspects: digital background, digital teaching reform, innovation trend of quality courses, and application scope of knowledge map. He introduced the construction model of the knowledge map, explained the role and interrelationship of "knowledge", "problem" and "ability" in the construction of the map; introduced the content of the subject knowledge map; An important application of the construction process, through the use of artificial intelligence, functions such as the establishment of a single learning space and the optimization of course resources can be achieved. Finally, through the typical school case of knowledge graph, the application of knowledge graph is shown to the teacher.

The college launched a training session on laboratory biosafety

On the morning of July 14, the college held a laboratory biosafety presentation and training meeting in the D209 conference room of the fourth complex building. Zhao Xiaodan, director of the International Division of the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs, was invited to give a lecture on the theme of "Strengthening the Biosafety Management of Animal Pathogenic Microbiology Laboratory" to the teachers and students present. The event was hosted by Professor Guo Aizhen of the college.

The expert group of the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs came to our school to carry out special inspections and training on laboratory biosafety

On July 13-14, the expert group of the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs carried out a special inspection on the biosafety of the highly pathogenic animal pathogenic microorganism laboratory of our school, and conducted a special inspection on the biosafety management regulations and policies of the veterinary laboratory and the main responsibility. Relevant teachers and students carry out special training.

The farewell event for the 2023 graduates of the Key Laboratory of Agricultural Animal Genetics, Breeding and Reproduction of the Ministry of Education was held

On the afternoon of June 23, the farewell event for the 2023 graduates of the Key Laboratory of Agricultural Animal Genetics, Breeding and Reproduction of the Ministry of Education was held in the F101 Lecture Hall of the Fourth Comprehensive Building. Mentors and graduate students from the Key Laboratory of Agricultural Animal Genetics, Breeding and Reproduction of the Ministry of Education attended the event.

Biosafety training and on-site simulation drills held in our hospital for the "Safety Production Month" series of activities

June this year is the 22nd national "safety production month", with the theme of "everyone pays attention to safety, everyone knows emergency". In order to firmly establish the safety awareness of teachers and students, build a "biological safety barrier", and create a strong safety atmosphere, at 15:00 on June 30, our hospital held a series of activities for the 2023 annual safety production month in the academic lecture hall of the Pig Healthy Breeding Collaborative Innovation Center Biosafety training and on-site simulation drills. Dr. Chen Xi, the safety director of Huazhong Agricultural University's third-level biosafety laboratory, gave a lecture entitled "Those Things in the Pathogenic Microbiology Laboratory" to the teachers and students present. More than 100 teachers and students from the college participated in the training, and the activity was hosted by Mr. Li Jingyu from the college office.

The college held the award ceremony of the Safety Production Month Safety Knowledge Competition

At 14:30 on the afternoon of June 27, the college held the award ceremony of the safety knowledge competition of the 2023 annual safety production month series activities in the lecture hall B202 of the fourth comprehensive building. Teacher Huang Yuanzhuo from the Safety Management Office of the Campus Construction and Security Department, the award-winning teachers, students and representatives of the college participated in the event..
